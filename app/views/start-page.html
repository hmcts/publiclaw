C110A Apply for a care, supervision or emergency protection order

Use this service to:

  • apply for care and supervision under Part 4 of the Children Act 1989
  • apply for other orders under Part 4 of the Children Act 1989
  • apply for emergency protection order under section 44 of the Children Act 1989

To use this service you would need to have an account with HM Courts and Tribunals services. Please contact HM Courts and Tribunals services if do not have an account with HM Courts and Tribunals services.

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
